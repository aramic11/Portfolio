About Me

Hi my name is Ahmed! I'm 25 years old and I enjoy many things. I love to play sports, travel the world, try new foods but most importantly challenge myself so I could grow. I graduated from UNH back in 2019 with a double major in Accounting and Finance. Since then I've worked as a staff accountant for Newforma for 3+ years and I've enjoyed it quite a lot. I learned a lot about business and what it takes to be successful as an organization. But something that I've always wanted to do is learn how to code so I could create my own apps and websites. After talking to a coworker and a couple of my friends, I discovered that UNH has a coding bootcamp. So I decided that the best time to try something new and grow is now while I'm still in my 20s. I know this will be a difficult journey but a fun and rewarding one as well. I cant wait to see what the future holds and I'm excited to learn and grow as a software developer.
